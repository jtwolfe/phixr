# Phixr Branding Guide

## Logo

The Phixr logo features a modern, geometric design with:
- **Circle and Cross Symbol** - Represents the intersection of technology and collaboration
- **Gradient Colors** - Teal to navy blue gradient symbolizing innovation and stability
- **"Phixr" Wordmark** - Bold, contemporary typography
- **"xr" Accent** - Extended reality / AI perspective indicator

**Files:**
- `assets/phixr.jpg` - Main logo (high resolution)

## Logo Usage

### Recommended Sizes
- **Large** (banner, hero): 300-500px wide
- **Medium** (documentation): 200-300px wide
- **Small** (icon, favicon): 64-128px wide

### Placement

```markdown
<div align="center">
  <img src="assets/phixr.jpg" alt="Phixr Logo" width="300" />
</div>
```

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Teal**: `#0892A5` (lighter, innovation)
- **Navy**: `#0D1D2B` (darker, stability)
- **Gradient**: Teal to Navy

**Secondary Colors:**
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` (backgrounds)
- **Light Gray**: `#F5F5F5` (accents)

## Typography

**Primary Font**: Modern, bold sans-serif
- Headlines and branding
- Strong, contemporary feel

**Secondary Font**: Clean, readable sans-serif
- Body text and documentation
- Excellent readability

## Voice & Tone

### Phixr Brand Voice
- **Friendly yet professional** - Approachable but credible
- **Direct and clear** - No jargon, clear explanations
- **Innovative** - Forward-thinking, cutting-edge
- **Collaborative** - Emphasizes teamwork with AI

### Example Messaging

❌ "Execute autonomous code generation in isolated environments"  
✅ "Code with your AI pair programmer in a secure sandbox"

❌ "Interface with Version Control Systems via webhook protocol"  
✅ "Connect to GitLab, GitHub, or Gitea with one click"

## Visual Elements

### Icons
- Use consistent, rounded corners (8px radius minimum)
- Primary color: Teal or Navy
- Secondary color: Light Gray
- Avoid thin strokes (2px minimum)

### Layouts
- **Generous whitespace** - Don't crowd content
- **Clear hierarchy** - Use size, color, weight intentionally
- **Alignment** - Left-align text, center logos
- **Grid system** - 8px baseline grid

## Do's and Don'ts

### ✅ Do's
- Use the logo on light backgrounds
- Maintain the aspect ratio
- Provide sufficient white space around the logo
- Use clear, readable font sizes
- Ensure sufficient contrast with background

### ❌ Don'ts
- Don't distort or stretch the logo
- Don't place on complex backgrounds
- Don't use outdated or faded colors
- Don't remove the wordmark for branding
- Don't use in very small sizes (< 64px)
